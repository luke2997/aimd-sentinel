Prototype · FDA/openFDA public evidence · AI medical-device governance

Source-cited governance dossiers for AI-enabled medical devices.

AIMD Sentinel links public FDA-style records, adverse-event narratives, recalls, enforcement data, and device-specific evidence gates to help hospital AI-governance and procurement teams review AI-enabled medical devices without overclaiming causality.

View headline demo Download validation CSV

What it does

Evidence gating

Separates high-confidence device-specific records from broad manufacturer, product-code, or generic-name matches.

Failure-theme extraction

Classifies narratives into software anomaly, calculation/classification, workflow, synchronization, hardware, and insufficient-information themes.

Governance dossiers

Generates procurement-ready reports with evidence summaries, limitations, vendor questions, and monitoring suggestions.

Important limitation

AIMD Sentinel is a governance and procurement-support prototype. It does not establish clinical or legal causality, does not estimate incidence rates, does not provide medical advice, and does not replace FDA, regulatory, clinical, or legal review. Public adverse-event records may be incomplete, duplicate, inaccurate, or unverified.
